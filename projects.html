Project 1 — Logic Gates

ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ

Project 1 was part of the SITE1101 course.
We constructed three basic logic gates — NOT, AND, and OR also (NAND, XOR)— using real hardware: breadboards, transistors (PN2222), resistors, jumpers, and LEDs.
The project required teamwork, lab visits, safety rules, and a full demonstration of how each gate works.
We also recorded and edited a video showing the build process and explanations.
