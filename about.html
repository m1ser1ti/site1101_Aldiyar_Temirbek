About Me

My name is Aldiyar Temirbek, and I come from the beautiful city of Astana, the capital of Kazakhstan. It’s a place where modern architecture meets endless steppe landscapes, and it’s the city where I grew up. I was raised in an environment that values education, hard work, and openness to the world qualities that I carry with me every day.

I am currently living and studying in Azerbaijan, at ADA University, majoring in Computer Science under the School of IT & Engineering (SITE). As a first-year student, this stage of my life is not just the beginning of my academic journey it’s the beginning of a much bigger path. Moving to a new country, adapting to a new environment, and meeting new people have already helped me grow both personally and academically.

Above this text, you can see photos of my country and my city places that shaped my character and worldview. Next to them, you will also find my personal photos that reflect who I am.

By nature, I am a very creative, active, and curious person. I find it difficult to sit still I’m always searching for new ideas, projects, and opportunities. I’m inspired by everything connected to technology, design, and creating something meaningful. I enjoy learning, experimenting, and challenging myself, and I see every task as a chance to grow.

I believe that my personality, determination, and passion for technology will help me build the future I aim for. This is just the beginning, and there is much more ahead.
