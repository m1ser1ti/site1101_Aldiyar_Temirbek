About Me

Hello! I’m Aldiyar Temirbek, a first-year student at ADA University. I am currently learning the fundamentals of information systems and web development in the SITE1101 course. This website is one of my first projects where I apply what I’ve learned about HTML, CSS, Git, and GitHub.

In the future, I hope to build more advanced websites, learn JavaScript in depth, and become more confident in software development. For now, I’m enjoying the process and improving day by day.
